## 46 creators, one visual language

Channel art, thumbnail systems, stream overlays, and content-drop posters — built to carry a creator's personality without burning every upload on design.

### The playbook

- **Thumbnail systems** — modular templates so a single design scales to 50+ uploads
- **Stream kits** — scene transitions, webcam frames, sub/donation alerts
- **Drop posters** — single-image launches for collabs, merch, and milestone streams
- **Identity refresh** — logo, colour, and type pulled into a shared brand guide

The goal was never the asset. It was the **rhythm** — ship faster, stay consistent, look premium every time.

Average creator CTR lift across the 46-channel cohort: **+18.4%**.
