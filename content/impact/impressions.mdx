## 120M+ impressions from design-led content

Across tournament broadcasts, creator uploads, and campaign drops, the design work we led compounded into **120 million+ on-platform impressions** - measured across YouTube, Instagram, X, and in-stream overlays.

### How the number broke down

- **YouTube** - 64M thumbnail impressions across creator channels
- **Instagram** - 38M reel cover + carousel impressions from tournament recaps
- **In-stream** - 12M broadcast impressions from sponsor bugs and mid-rolls
- **X / Twitter** - 6M from match posters, result cards, and poll graphics

> Every pixel had a job. Thumbnails had to win the scroll, broadcast bugs had to read at 720p, posters had to survive Instagram's compression.

Design as a **distribution multiplier**, not a decoration pass.
