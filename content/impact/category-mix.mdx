## Where the work goes

A year-in-review breakdown of every deliverable shipped — grouped by deliverable type, not by client. Useful when prospective partners ask *"what actually happens in those six months?"*.

### The split

- **Tournament graphics** — 34% of output, highest velocity
- **Creator kits** — 26% of output, highest repeat-engagement
- **Campaign launches** — 18% of output, highest revenue leverage
- **Brand identities** — 12% of output, longest-tail impact
- **Motion / broadcast** — 10% of output, highest per-asset polish

> Tournaments pay the bills. Identity work pays the referrals.

The ratio shifts each quarter — this is the shape of a **busy, retainer-heavy year**, not the only shape.
