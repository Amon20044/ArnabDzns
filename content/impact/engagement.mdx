## +41% engagement across the cohort

Average comment + save + share rate across the 46-creator cohort climbed **41%** vs. their pre-collaboration baseline.

### The levers

- **Covers that promise a payoff** — the thumbnail tells the story the video completes
- **Hook frames** — first three seconds designed as a visual, not an afterthought
- **Carousel architecture** — slide 1 opens, slide 2 hooks, last slide converts
- **Caption-to-design alignment** — the visual never fights the copy

> Engagement isn't a style problem. It's a **clarity problem** — make the value obvious, early.

The design brief always started with: *what does the viewer save this for?*
