## Live streaming collaborations that felt native on broadcast

The live-collab work sat in the hardest middle ground: sponsor requirements had to be visible, the stream identity had to stay intact, and every graphic still needed to read in motion.

### What counted as a collaboration

- **Broadcast overlay packs** for tournament day streams
- **Sponsor-ready countdown and lower-third kits**
- **Live score, MVP, and results graphics**
- **Creator co-stream visual packages** tuned for smaller facecam layouts

### Why the number mattered

- **18 live streaming collaborations** meant the system held up across different production teams
- The strongest packages were the ones that worked at multiple resolutions without redesign
- Reusable components reduced revision pressure during match days and launch nights

> Broadcast design only looks effortless when the system underneath it is disciplined.

The goal was never to make a static poster look good on stream. The goal was to make the entire stream feel branded, intentional, and sponsor-safe in real time.
