## Before vs. after: a campaign rebuild

A mid-tier esports org came in with a campaign that was underperforming its ad spend. We kept the product, the channels, and the budget identical - and rebuilt the **visual system end to end**.

### The swap

- **Key-art** - redrawn with a single hero focal point instead of three competing ones
- **Thumbnails** - tightened crops, larger typography, a consistent red-to-violet ramp
- **Landing page** - cut from four scrolls to one, CTA above the fold
- **Reel covers** - unified across the 8-video launch so the algorithm could pattern-match

### What changed

| Metric | Before | After |
|:--|:--|:--|
| Weekly reach | 1.8M | 5.8M |
| CTR | 3.8% | 4.5% |
| Saves | 3.4K | 12.1K |
| Bounce rate | 61% | 38% |

> Same budget. Same channels. **3.2x reach**.

Creative debt is usually cheaper to pay down than media spend.
