## Built the visual backbone for 28 tournaments

From grassroots showdowns to broadcast-grade finals, every tournament arrived with its own identity, sponsor deck, and creator roster. I designed **hero key-art, bracket graphics, lower-thirds, stage loops, and post-match recap cards** that held up at 4K on stream and across social.

### What that meant on the ground

- **Brand kits shipped** — logo lockups, colour systems, type hierarchy per event
- **Broadcast-ready templates** — OBS scenes with animated transitions and on-air tickers
- **Sponsor deliverables** — mid-roll bumpers, leaderboard bugs, trophy reveals
- **Recap packages** — end-of-day highlight thumbnails, winner cards, aftermovie titles

> "The first time a sponsor flagged our stream graphics as 'broadcast quality' — that was the turning point."

Most organizers came back for their next season, and four referred partner leagues before their own event even wrapped.
