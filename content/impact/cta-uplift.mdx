## +18.4% CTA uplift across drops

Redesigned landing pages, drop posters, and launch reels for six campaigns. Measured uplift against each client's own 90-day baseline — not a cherry-picked benchmark.

### What moved the needle

- **Hierarchy first** — one headline per screen, one action, zero competing focal points
- **Motion as a signal** — subtle entry on the primary CTA so the eye locks on it
- **Copy + design pairing** — shorter verbs, stronger verbs, tested against existing CTR
- **Mobile-first sizing** — tap targets hit Apple's 44×44pt minimum without exception

> Baseline CTR **3.8%** → post-redesign **4.5%**. Same traffic, same product, different surface.

Small number, big signal: design choices that **earn clicks** are worth revisiting every quarter.
